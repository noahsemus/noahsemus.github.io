GMessage is project borne out of pure passion and utter frustration. I am not shy about my love for Google, and I have always had a passion about making ubiquitous experiences (like messaging) easier and more fun to use. Google's troubles in this space opened up a perfect opportunity for me. However, frustration has come from my education at Pratt Institute. Wanting to be an interactive designer, I've found Pratt almost refuses to teach me and give me projects that I think are important to becoming well versed in the digital space. Therefore, I created my own project -- not only to teach myself, but to show I know what I can do.
